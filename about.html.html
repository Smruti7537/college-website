About Us

Best College In Bhubaneswar.

Trident is one of the Prominent college under Biju Pattnaik University Of Technology (BPUT). Trident Group Of Institutions has 2 parts under it Trident Academy of Technology (Specially for B.Tech & M.Tech) Trident Academy of Creative Technology (For BCA, MCA, BBA & MBA). Trident Academy of Professional Studies ( B.Sc Biotech & M.Sc Biotech) Faculties are best & helpful. Best Lab & Library. Campus placements are good. On-campus & Off-campus placements are organized by the institution. During the campus time college provides training to groom the students to be able to clear the campus. Top MNCs like Capgemini, Deloitte, Infosys, TCS, Cognizant, Wipro & many more companies come to hire college graduates. This college can be referred to all the students who are good in IT & want to be placed in a top MNC. Lab faculties are also helpful & clear doubts when asked. Hostel facilities also available. Campus is well secured & is always under 24/7 surveillance. Canteen also provides healthy food & price is cheaper as per the market. Clean canteen. Spacious parking. College allows to play sports & participate in Inter-college competitions. Transportation services are also available. Teachers & faculties are the best in colleges. They always help to clear the doubts.
